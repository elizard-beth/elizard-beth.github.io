More information on chocobot

Chocobot is a discord.js discord bot created around late 2020 and early 2021 and designed for Chococord.
The latest version of chocobot is v2.5.9, which was released around November 2021 and has been maintained since then.
Chocobot was designed originally for moderation, but later added fun features and commands.
